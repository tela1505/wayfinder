Step 3: Learn relevant skills

To stand out from the crowd, it is important to identify which skills will help you excel and succeed in a career as a mathematician. The skills we believe are highly desirable for this role are:


  • Fundamentals of mathematics
  • Applications of mathematics (how to use mathematical skills in the real world)

Additional knowledge in the following areas may help too:


  • Useful knowledge on any topic you find interesting! (Mathematics can be applied to a wide variety of use cases, from business to medicine) Learn more about what excites you and how you can apply mathematics to make it even better!
  • Coding fundamentals (much easier than you think!)


For each of these skills and domain areas, we have compiled a list of relevant resources that we believe will help you out a lot! They are fun, short and sweet videos that do a great job of explaining all the basic concepts required.
