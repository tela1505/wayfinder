Step 3: Learn relevant skills

To stand out from the crowd, it is important to identify which skills will help you excel and succeed in a fashion management career. The skills we believe are highly desirable for this role are:


  • Negotiation
  • Time Management
  • Stock Management

Additional knowledge in the following areas may help too:


  • Basic business knowledge (supply and demand)
  • Design and the creative fashion process

For each of these skills and domain areas, we have compiled a list of relevant resources that we believe will help you out a lot! They are fun, short and sweet videos that do a great job of explaining all the basic concepts required.
