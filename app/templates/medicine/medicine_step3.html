Step 3: Learn relevant skills

To stand out from the crowd, it is important to identify which skills will help you excel and succeed in a medical career. The skills we believe are highly desirable for this role are:


  • The core principles bodily functions (an appreciation for human Biology!)
  • The fundamentals of the scientific method (hypothesising, testing, validating etc.)
  • Communication
  • Empathy (how to understand how others are feeling)

Additional knowledge in the following areas may help too:


  • Basic Mathematical understanding
  • Time management
  • Decision making (often quickly and confidently)


For each of these skills and domain areas, we have compiled a list of relevant resources that we believe will help you out a lot! They are fun, short and sweet videos that do a great job of explaining all the basic concepts required.
